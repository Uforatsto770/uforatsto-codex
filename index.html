Setup Tiers

Three clear setup levels.

Small Setup Pilot

For pilot runs, testing, and smaller-footprint production.

Example scale: 4x4 or 4x8 tent.

  • Compact tent or starter room shell
  • Basic climate and airflow package
  • Starter sensors and controls
  • Install-ready early production layout
Medium Setup Commercial

For operators who need more output and tighter control.

Example scale: 8x8 or 10x10 tent, or a dedicated room.

  • Multi-tent layout or dedicated room build

  • Installation planning with upgrade path
Industrial Setup Facility

  • Integrated climate and automation package
  • Commissioning and turnkey rollout
  • Retrofit and capacity expansion planning
